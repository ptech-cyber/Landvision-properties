Who We Are

Trusted Land Brokers in Kakamega

Landvision Properties was founded with one mission — to make land ownership simple, transparent, and accessible for every Kenyan.

  • We help clients buy and sell verified plots with clean title deeds.
  • We conduct professional title deed verification and land searches.
  • We offer property management and real estate consulting services.

Founded and led by Gregory Luyeku, Landvision Properties has grown into one of Kakamega’s most reputable land brokerage firms, trusted by individuals, families, and investors across Kenya.
